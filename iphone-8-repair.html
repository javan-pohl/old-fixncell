iPhone 8 Touchscreen Non-Responsive

When the phone screen is no longer responsing to your touch, you may need to get your screen replaced. The touchscreen is an element known as a "digitizer" (a device that converts physical inputs into digital signals), that is a component of your iPhone 8's LCD screen assembly. Fortunately, the process we use at Fix N cell to repair broken iPhone screens is to replace the entire screen assembly. If your iPhone 8 touchscreen is not working, we can fix it for you with an iPhone screen repair. Often the touchscreen will not work properly if the screen is cracked or damaged. However, if there is no physical damage to your phone or screen, you can try performing a hard reset to restore touch functionality.

One Year Warranty on iPhone 8 Screen Repairs

When you get your broken iPhone 8 screen fixed at FixNcell phone repair in Westminster, CO, you receive a 12 month warranty on your screen replacement. The warranty covers functionality issues such as the touchscreen not working properly or the LCD not showing the image correctly (lines of distortion or blank spots in the image, not simply colors apprearing slightly different than before). The warranty does not cover physical damage to your new iPhone 8 screen. If the glass on your new screen is cracked or chipped, the warranty does not apply. Instances of the iPhone 8 glass separating from the plastic frame are treated on a case-by-case basis.
